Cheap Mans Ramen

Quick, Easy, and Cheap

Tired of plain ole instant ramen? Well look no more! With just a few ingredients from the kitchen this ramen will be bussin

Ingredients

  • Passion
  • 2 Instant Ramem Packs
  • 1 Egg
  • Onion and Garlic Powder
  • Cilantro

Instructions

  1. Cook up the ramen how you prefer
  2. Fry 1 egg
  3. Add as much seasoning as you like
  4. Add egg to top off ramen bowl and enjoy
